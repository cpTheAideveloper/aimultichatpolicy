Overview

AI MultiChat is a Chrome extension that allows users to send prompts to multiple AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek) from a single control panel. It manages browser tabs and injects user prompts into supported AI chat pages.

This privacy policy explains how AI MultiChat handles user data.

What Information We Collect

‚úÖ We do NOT collect or store any personal information.

  • No authentication credentials
  • No chat history or AI conversation content
  • No device or location data
  • No behavioral or analytics tracking

How the Extension Works

AI MultiChat runs entirely in your browser. It uses Chrome APIs to:

  • Open and manage tabs for supported AI platforms
  • Inject your prompts into the native input field of each AI model
  • Store UI preferences locally via chrome.storage.local

No information is sent to external servers.
